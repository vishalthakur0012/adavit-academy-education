PTE Training

Study Plan

Candidates are tested in listening, reading, writing and speaking. All candidates take the same Listening and Speaking tests and a choice of Reading and Writing tests- Academic or General Training. The first three tests – Listening, Reading and Writing- must be completed in one day. No break is given between the three tests. The Speaking test may be taken, at the discretion of the test centre, in the period of seven days before or after the other three tests.

What will you learn from this course?

In an IELTS Test Format Course, you can expect to learn a range of skills and strategies that are essential for successfully navigating and performing well on the International English Language Testing System (IELTS) exam.

  • Listening Skills
  • Reading Skills
  • Writing Skills
  • Speaking Skills
  • Test-Taking Strategies
  • Vocabulary and Grammar

Details about the course

An IELTS Test Format Course is designed to help you prepare for the International English Language Testing System (IELTS) exam. The IELTS exam assesses your English language proficiency and is commonly used for study, work, and immigration purposes in English-speaking countries.
